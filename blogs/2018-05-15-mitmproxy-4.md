We’ve just released mitmproxy v4.0 , and it’s an absolute corker. Among the usual long list of bugfixes and improvements, one thing stands out: speed. Users should see about 4x improvement in core request throughput for mitmdump, and a 10x or more improvement for mitmproxy console.
