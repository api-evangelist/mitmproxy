Mitmproxy has participated in the Google Summer of Code program under the umbrella of the Honeynet Project since 2012. The students that have come to us through GSoC have had a deep and lasting impact on our project - no less than two of mitmproxy’s current co-leaders started their journey as GSoC students. For the right student, GSOC is a wonderful opportunity to be mentored to become a full-fledged mitmproxy dev team member.
